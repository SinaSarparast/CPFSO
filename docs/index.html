What's Cooking?

COMP 6237 : Data Mining

Course work1 - Team and Project Brief

Description

We come across many ingredients in our day to day life that are used to make delicious recipes. The purpose of this project is to explore the available recipes dataset containing over 400,000 recipes, which includes a recipe ID, cuisine type, and a list of ingredients for each recipe. This data will allow us to perform analysis, modelling and visualization of various aspects and features about recipes, their ingredients and the cuisines they belong to.

------------------QUESTIONS HERE------------------------------------------------

We believe that this concept will help us to use a range of data mining techniques and demonstrate the stages of the data mining pipeline. We will explore:

  • The prediction of cuisine from ingredients
  • Other interesting patterns within recipe ingredients
